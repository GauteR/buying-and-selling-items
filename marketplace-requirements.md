# Requirements and limits per marketplace

Use this file when tailoring listing suggestions to a specific marketplace. Limits and rules may change. When a limit or rule is uncertain, look it up yourself (web search or fetch the platform’s help/terms); do not ask the user to check.

---

## Finn (Norway)

- **Language/currency:** Norwegian; NOK.
- **Title:** Required. Finn does not publish a maximum character count; keep the title short and searchable (brand, model, type). Avoid caps lock and spam wording.
- **Description:** Plain text. If character limits are needed, look up current limits at [Finn.no](https://www.finn.no). Describe condition, dimensions and any defects clearly.
- **Images:** Standard image pack typically 1–8 images; extended pack up to around 20 (product-dependent). Recommendation: 5–10 good images for simple items; 10–29 for cars and larger items. Use daylight, sharp images, simple background; avoid filters and generic stock photos.
- **Category:** Required; choose the closest subcategory. Location affects visibility.
- **Price:** NOK. State whether price is fixed or “open to swap”.
- **Rules to mention:** Be clear on returns (e.g. “no returns” or “see photos”). Finn has rules against misleading ads; describe flaws and wear.
- **Prohibited/restrictions:** Look up [Finn terms and rules](https://www.finn.no/om-finn/vilkar) when needed; typically prohibited: illegal items, counterfeits, misleading information, and infringement of third-party rights.
- **Tone/length:** Short and factual; Norwegian. Location and category matter for visibility.

## eBay

- **Language/currency:** Depends on site (e.g. ebay.com, ebay.no); currency follows (USD, NOK, etc.).
- **Title:** Max **80 characters** (including spaces and punctuation). The first 50–55 characters often show on mobile; put the most important keywords first. Avoid ALL CAPS, filler words and special characters. Include brand, model, colour, type.
- **Description:** Max 500,000 characters. Recommended length 200–800 words. Many sites support HTML; look up the specific eBay site for current format. Describe condition, defects and photo references.
- **Images:** Up to 24 images per listing (free). Recommend at least 6–12 images showing angles and any defects. Video supported.
- **Category:** Required; choose a specific category and Item Specifics where available.
- **Price:** Currency follows the eBay site. Can set fixed price or auction. Subtitle (55 characters) is optional for a fee.
- **Rules to mention:** State return policy (e.g. 30 days, no returns). Seller rating and description quality affect buyer trust.
- **Prohibited/restrictions:** Look up [eBay listing policies](https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/listing-policies/listing-policies?id=4340) when needed; prohibited include illegal items, counterfeits, misleading listings and trademark infringement.
- **Tone/length:** More detailed and international; good descriptions and many images are expected.

## Facebook Marketplace

- **Language/currency:** Follows the user’s region; language and currency are local (e.g. Norwegian/NOK in Norway).
- **Title:** Recommended about 50–70 characters. Include category, key feature, brand/model/size.
- **Description:** **30–9,999 characters**. Plain text only; no HTML or links. Use sentence case; avoid excessive punctuation. The description must match the title and image. Do not include company info, delivery details or contact info in the description. The first 140 characters matter for search; put condition and key benefits at the start.
- **Images:** At least one image required. Look up [Facebook Marketplace help](https://www.facebook.com/help/811082570742714) for current image rules and limits when needed.
- **Category:** Choose the category that matches buyer filters.
- **Price:** State price in local currency. Location is used for local matching.
- **Rules to mention:** Be clear on condition and any “as is” sale. Avoid links and advertising in the description.
- **Prohibited/restrictions:** Follow [Facebook Commerce Policy](https://www.facebook.com/policies/commerce); prohibited include illegal items, misleading listings and rights infringement.
- **Tone/length:** Short and informal; local focus. Clear condition and price matter most.

## Blocket (Sweden)

- **Language/currency:** Swedish (or Danish, Norwegian, English for private listings; business ads must be in Swedish); SEK.
- **Title:** The heading must describe the item or service. No company names, links or unnecessary characters in the heading.
- **Description:** The listing text must describe the specific item or service. No links-only to other pages; no copying from other ads. No keywords that create misleading search hits or unauthorised use of trademarks. Trade/swap offers must not appear in the listing text (causes misleading hits). Look up Blocket’s help for current character limits when needed.
- **Images:** JPG or PNG; min 20 KB, max 20 MB. Vehicle ads: up to 50 images; the first 27 are shown on Blocket.se. Recommended size about 1024×768 px. Images with added text are not allowed.
- **Category:** Choose the appropriate category.
- **Price:** SEK. State if trade/swap is wanted etc. outside the description text where it does not create misleading search hits.
- **Rules to mention:** Be clear on condition and any “as is” sale.
- **Prohibited/restrictions:** Look up [Blocket advertising rules](https://www.blocket.se/om/villkor/annonseringsregler) when needed. Listings only in Swedish, Danish, Norwegian or English; no duplicates of the same item.
- **Tone/length:** Short and clear; Swedish. Similar use case to Finn in Norway.

## Others (DBA, Tori, national equivalents)

- **Language/currency:** Adapt language and currency to the platform (e.g. DBA: Danish/DKK; Tori: Finnish/EUR).
- **Title:** Keep short and descriptive; look up the platform’s help for character limits when needed.
- **Description:** Honest and clear; mention defects and condition. Look up whether plain text or HTML is allowed.
- **Images:** Look up the platform’s requirements (count, format, size) when needed.
- **Category/location:** Choose the right category and location where available.
- **Price:** State in the platform’s currency.
- **Rules to mention:** Clear returns/condition in line with the platform’s norms.
- **Prohibited/restrictions:** Look up the platform’s terms before suggesting publish-ready text.
- **Tone/length:** Adapt to the platform (short/local vs. more detailed).
